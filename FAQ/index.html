Frequently Asked Questions


What is the best way to determine my suit size?

The best way to determine your suit size is by measuring your chest, waist, and inseam. You can refer to our sizing chart for accurate measurements. If you're unsure, we also offer a virtual fitting service where you can consult with our experts online.

How do I care for my suit to keep it looking its best?

Proper suit care is essential for maintaining its quality and longevity. We recommend dry cleaning your suit only when necessary to prevent damage to the fabric. Additionally, hang your suit on a sturdy hanger after each wear to allow wrinkles to fall out naturally, and use a garment brush or steamer to refresh the fabric.

Can I customize my suit with specific features or alterations?

Yes, we offer customization options for many of our suits, including fabric choices, lining options, and alterations such as sleeve length or trouser hemming. Simply contact our customer service team to discuss your preferences, and we'll do our best to accommodate your requests.

What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your suit, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Please ensure the suit is in its original condition with tags attached.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on your location. At checkout, you'll be able to select your country for shipping options and estimated delivery dates.
